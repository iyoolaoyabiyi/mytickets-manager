## Naming convention

|Type|Pattern|Example(s)|Notes|
|---|---|---|---|
|Block (Component)|`c-<name>`|`c-button`, `c-card`, `c-hero`|Top-level component scope.|
|Element|`__<part>`|`c-card__title`, `c-hero__cta`|Sub-parts of a block only.|
|Modifier|`--<variant>`|`c-button--primary`, `c-tag--open`|Visual/behavioral variants.|
|State hooks|`is-<state>` or `data-state="<state>"`|`is-loading`, `data-state="open"`|Use for transient UI states.|
|A11y hooks|ARIA / roles / data attrs|`role="status"`, `aria-expanded`|Prefer semantic HTML first.|

## Composition Maps (Skeleton)

### Base Layout: `base-layout`

- `l-container`
	- `c-skiplink` > `a11y.skiplink`
	- `c-header`
		- `__brand`
			- `h1` | `img` > `app.name`
		- `__menu-toggle` > button(icon) > `nav.label.*`: a11y label
		- `__nav`
			- `__link` > \[`nav.login`, `nav.signup`] or if auth \[`nav.dashboard`, `nav.tickets`, `nav.logout` ]
			- `__theme-toggle` > button(icon) > `theme.label`: a11y label
	- `c-toast` > `toast.*`
	- `main#main-content`
	- `c-footer`
		- `__legal` > `footer.copyright`
		- `__links` 
			- `a` > \[`footer.links.*`]
		- `__note` > `footer.note`

### Dashboard Layout (Dashboard and Tickets Screens): `dash-layout`

- \#base-layout
	- `l-grid`
		- `c-dash-nav`
			- `__link` > link(icon) > \[`nav.dashboard`, `nav.tickets]
			- `__button` > button(icon) > `nav.new`
		- `div#dash-content`

### Landing (`/`): `landing-page`

- \#base-layout
	- `c-hero`
		- `__wave`: decorative background svg
		- `__circle-1`: decorative top right svg
		- `__circle-2`: decorative top left svg
		- `l-stack`
			- `__title` > `landing.hero.title`
			- `__subtitle` > `landing.hero.subtitle`
			- `__actions` 
				- `c-button` > \[`landing.cta.primary`, `landing.cta.secondary`] or if auth `landing.cta.dashboard`
	- `c-feature-container`
		- `c-feature-card` \[`track`, `organize`, `resolve`]
			- `__icon` > icon: decorative svg icon
			- `__title` > `features.*.title`
			- `__body` > `features.*.body`
	- `c-step-container`
		- `c-step-card` \[`one`, `two`, `three`]
			- `__icon`: decorative element
			- `__title` > `steps.*.title`
			- `__body` > `steps.*.body`

### Login (`/auth/login`): `login-page`

- \#base-layout
	- `c-auth-container`
		- `c-page-header`
			- `__title` > `auth.heading`
			- `__subtitle` > `auth.tagline`
		- `c-auth-card`
				- `__title` > `auth.login.title`
			- `c-auth-form--login` > 
				- `l-stack`
					- `c-field` \[`email`, `password`]
							- `__label` > `form.auth.*`
						- `__control` > `form.auth.*.placeholder`: as placeholder
						- `__message` > `validation`
				- `c-button` > `form.login.submit`
				- `__switch` > `auth.login.switch`
				- `__hint` > `auth.login.demo`

### Signup (`/auth/signup`): `signup-page`

- same as Login with adjusted copy
	- fields {`name`, `email`, `password`, `confirmpassword`}
	- no hint
	- replace `login` with `signup`

### Dashboard (`/dashboard`): `dashboard-page`

- \#dash-layout
	- `l-stack`
		- `c-page-header`
			- `__title` > `dashboard.title`
			- `__subtitle` > `dashboard.subtitle`
		- `c-stat-container`
			- `c-stat-card` \[`open`, `closed`, `in_progress`]
				- `__icon`
				- `__label` > `dashboard.stats.*`
				- `__value`
		- `c-button` > `dashboard.to.tickets`
		- %% if no tickets %%
		- `c-empty--primary`
			- `__icon`
			- `__title` > `tickets.empty.primary`
			- `__action` `c-button` > `tickets.empty.primary.action`

### Tickets (`/tickets`): `tickets-page`

- \#dash-layout
	- `c-page-header`
		- `__title` > `tickets.title`
	- `c-filters` 
		- `__search` `l-cluster`
			- `c-field` 
				- `__label` > `tickets.filters.search`
				- `__control` > `tickets.filters.search.placeholder`
			- `c-button` > `tickets.filters.search`
		- `l-cluster`
			- \[`__status`, `__priority`]
				- `c-field` \[`status`, `priority`]
					- `__label` > `ticket.*` 
					- `__control` > `ticket.*.*options`: eg, `ticket.status.open`
	- `l-stack`
		- Either `c-empty--primary`
			- `__icon`
			- `__title` > `tickets.empty.primary`
			- `__action` `c-button` > `tickets.empty.primary.action`
		- Or `c-empty--filtered`
			- `__icon`
			- `__title` > `tickets.empty.filtered`
			- `__action` `c-button` > `tickets.empty.filtered.action`
		- Or `c-tickets-container`
			- `c-ticket-card`
				- `__title`
				- `__description`
				- `__status`
				- `__priority`
				- `__actions` `l-cluster` \[`edit`, `delete`]
					- `c-button` > `ticket.*`
	- `c-modal` \[`create`, `update`] (hidden until open)
		- `__title` > `ticket.*`
		- `__body` `c-ticket-form` \[`create`, `update`] 
			- `l-stack`
				- `c-field` \[`title`, `description`]
					- `__label` > `ticket.*`
					- `__control` > `form.ticket.*.placeholder`: as placeholder
					- `__message` > `validation`
				- `l-cluster`
					- `c-field` \[`status`, `priority`]
						- `__label` > `ticket.*`
						- `__control` > `ticket.*.*options`: as placeholder
			- `l-cluster` \[`submit`, `cancel`]
				- `c-button` > \[`form.ticket.*.submit`, `form.cancel`]
	- `c-modal`
		- `__title` > `ticket.delete.title`
		- `__body` > `confirm.delete.body`
		- `__actions` 
			- `c-button` \[`confirm.delete.confirm`, `confirm.delete.cancel`]
